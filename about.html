About Myself

I am Ashraf Ali Suman, born in the village of Tetaiya, under the Dirai sub-district of Sunamganj district. My father is Alhaj Abdul Bachit Talukdar. I completed my primary education at the government school in my village. After that, I studied at Kulanji Darusunnah Hafizia Madrasah for my primary education, and then I pursued higher education at Jamia Islamia Darul Ulum Alaitali and Katia Madrasah, where I obtained the highest degree from a Qawmi madrasa, equivalent to a Master's degree today. Additionally, I successfully completed my Quranic studies with distinction from Anjuman-e-Taleem-ul-Quran Bangladesh Board and have extensively studied Bengali literature and writing. I also hold a diploma in computer studies.
